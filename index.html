About Us

WorldLaw.ai is an international legal research startup. We aim to democratize access to international legal knowledge through advanced, AI-powered search tools that make complex legal information easy to navigate.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading platform for international legal research, competing directly with established players like Jus Mundi. We believe that legal research should be accessible, fast, and reliable for professionals and institutions worldwide.

Our Products

WorldLaw Chat: An AI-powered legal assistant to help users with international law questions and research, launching soon at chat.worldlaw.ai.

Advanced Legal Search: A search tool designed for faster insights into treaties, case law, and regulations across the globe.
